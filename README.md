• MVC 5 is great for applications where you want to leverage server-side features, built-in security, and faster initial development for traditional, page-based websites.
Best Suited For:Traditional websites, CRUD applications, and content-heavy sites.

• Angular is preferred for high-interactivity applications (like dashboards, social media, or complex management portals) that require a "desktop-like" experience in the browser.
Best Suited For:Large-scale, complex enterprise systems requiring strict standards and high team synchronization.

• Vue is the progressive choice for developers who want a balance between the simplicity of traditional web development and the high-interactivity of modern SPAs, offering a highly flexible, lightweight, and easy-to-learn structure that scales with your project needs
Best Suited For: Projects needing a balance between fast development, modern performance, and ease of maintenance.
